Visualization Results on Anatomy-Sentence Alignment

Anatomy-Sentence Alignment has two different ways to handle the corresponding report and image.
The first is Merge Bbox. We freezed the sentences and merging the related bounding box to close to the sentence.
The second is Split Sent. We freezed the bounding box and split the sentence into the related bbox to make them more closer.

What follows is an example. And the original image and the total detected bounding box are shown in the figure. The report corresponding to the original image is below the image.
The area and text corresponding to each bounding box can be viewed through the drop-down menu.
